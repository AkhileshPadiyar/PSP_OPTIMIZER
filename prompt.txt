## Optimizer Requirements

The scheduling algorithm must match the architecture exactly.

* Select the six cheapest hours for pumping.
* Select the six most expensive hours for generation.
* Pump 100 MWh.
* Generate 100 MWh by consuming 125 MWh of stored water (80% round-trip efficiency).
* Respect minimum and maximum storage limits.
* Produce exactly 24 schedule entries.
* Store the results in the database exactly as specified.

Do not replace this algorithm with another optimization technique.
